So, what is a Campus Ambassador?


Campus Ambassadors are representatives of all the colleges participating in Effervescence. They play a pivotal role in providing maximum coverage for the fest, by spreading the word amongst their peers through various social media and providing a medium for communication between their institution and IIITA. With time, each Campus Ambassador becomes an integral part of the Effervescence family, and are regularly in touch with the team operating in IIITA. Each CA is tasked with providing maximum publicity, both offline and online, of Effe in their respective institution and encourage active participation from students of all years.
